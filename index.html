Island Hopper

Introduction

Island Hopper is an abominable parody of PlayerUnknown's Battlegounds in the style of Dwarf Fortress with inspiration from Team Fortress 2

In the year 2400, devastating war leaves dying civilizations fighting desperately for the last remaining inhabitable places on Earth - remote islands in the middle of the ocean. You, a most unfortunate civilian with absolutely no preparation for armed combat, are sent to fight for your country on one of these islands. Will you survive?

Weird weapons

Discover a variety of weird, unidentified weapons that you've never seen before. See if you can figure out what each Metal Pipe Device does! And be careful not to shoot yourself in the foot!

Development

Island Hopper is a project supported entirely by one developer's free time. As such, it is a work in progress.
